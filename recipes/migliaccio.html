Migliaccio

Description

another recipe vvv found on youtube lol

Ingredients

  • 3 1/2 tbsp (50g) butter
  • 1 qt (1L) milk
  • 1 vanilla bean or 1 tsp extract
  • 1 lemon peel
  • 1 1/4c (230g) semolina
  • 6 eggs
  • 1 1/2c (310g) sugar
  • 1c (250g) ricotta
  • powdered sugar and lemon for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat butter, milk, lemon peel, salt, and vanilla to a simmer.
  2. Remove solids, then turn the head down and add in the semolina.
  3. Stir for a minute or two until mixture becomes a mush.
  4. Set aside to cool.
  5. Mix together eggs and sugar until light and creamy.
  6. Then, mix in ricotta and semolina until smooth.
  7. Butter a springform pan and pour in the mixture, bake at around 350F or 170C for an hour until a bit jiggly in the center.
  8. Let the cake slowly come to room temperature before removing the springform pan, then top with powdered sugar, garnish with a lemon and enjoy!
