Gising-gising

Description

A spicy Filipino dish that will truly gising you (wake you up)!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb green beans Baguio beans, chopped (about 3/4 inches)
  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons shrimp paste
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 medium onion minced
  • tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 pieces chili chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Saut√© garlic and onion
  3. Add pork. Cook for 3 to 5 minutes or until color turns medium brown
  4. Put-in ground black pepper, shrimp paste, and chili. Stir.
  5. Pour-in coconut milk. Let boil and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Add the green beans. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes while stirring gradually.
  7. Turn off heat. Serve with white rice.
  8. Share and enjoy!
