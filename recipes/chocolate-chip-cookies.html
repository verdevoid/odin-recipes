Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This is the only non-Filipino dish, the universally loved chocolate chip cookies. This version uses browned butter. There's no need for a mixer, nor creaming the butter and sugar in this recipe. It also uses a hint of coffee but does not taste like it. This is a recipe I stumbled across while watching YouTube shorts from Baking with Zoha and many have loved it.
